Two Approaches to NIS2 Implementation

Approach 1: Reactive Implementation

  • Wait for local enforcement to begin
  • Focus primarily on minimum compliance requirements
  • Treat as a regulatory burden to be managed
  • Implement quickly under pressure
  • View as a cost center with limited business value

Approach 2: Strategic Implementation

  • Begin planning while requirements are crystallizing
  • Integrate compliance with broader business objectives
  • Leverage compliance for competitive advantage
  • Implement systematically with proper planning
  • View as an investment in operational excellence

Both approaches achieve compliance.

The difference is in the business value created and the implementation experience.

Strategic organizations use compliance initiatives to strengthen their overall business capabilities.
